Your Path to Advocacy Excellence

If you've ever dreamed of captivating a courtroom and developing your advocacy skills, Mooting and Mock Trial is the activity for you. It teaches you real-life skills needed to be a lawyer, like analysing problems, crafting arguments, and delivering them with poise.

Legal Analysis

Master the art of dissecting complex legal problems and identifying key issues

Argument Crafting

Develop compelling legal arguments backed by solid research and precedent

Oral Advocacy

Deliver powerful presentations with confidence and professional poise

Proven Success

LawSoc has a storied history of success in Mooting; recent teams have placed 2nd place in the National Moot, as well as victories at recent Intervarsity moots.
