About the Competition

LawSoc is proud to run the Junior Schools Debating Competition for another year. This competition, a long-standing tradition, is entirely organised and judged by LawSoc members.

It serves to promote competitive debating among secondary school students in 1st to 3rd Year, with weekly debates against their peers, fostering critical thinking and public speaking skills.
