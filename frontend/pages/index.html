Welcome to the Law Society of UCD

The UCD Law Society is the largest student society in UCD, and is one of the oldest and most pre-eminent societies in Ireland.

We serve as the faculty society for the UCD Sutherland School of Law, as well as one of UCD's two debating unions.

A Century of Service to UCD Students

Since 1911, LawSoc has been a leading student society, both with law students and the general student body.

LawSoc has seen some of the most popular events in UCD student society history, ranging from house debates to awarding of Honorary Lifetime Memberships.

For over a century, LawSoc has been serving the law faculty of UCD, and this year is no different, with many events coming in the near future.
