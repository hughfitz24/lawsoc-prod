In Brief

A Call for Submissions

The UCD Law Society proudly presents In Brief, a new platform for legal discussion, analysis, and scholarly contributions from students and legal professionals.

We invite you to be part of our inaugural submissions. Whether you wish to examine contemporary legal debates, offer a critique of landmark cases, or explore emerging areas of law, we welcome your contributions.

Submission Guidelines

We welcome submissions from students and professionals, regardless of background or experience. Submissions should be original, well-researched, and written in a clear, professional manner.

  • Opinion pieces on pressing legal issues
  • Case law analysis & case notes
  • Legislative reviews
  • Legal history and theory

How to Submit

Please ensure your submission aligns with the guidelines above. We encourage diverse topics related to law.

To submit your article, email us at auditor@lawsoc.ie.
