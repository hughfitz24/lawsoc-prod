In Brief

A Call for Submissions

The Law Society of UCD proudly presents In Brief, a new platform for legal discussion, insightful analysis, and scholarly contributions from students and legal professionals alike.

We invite you to be part of our inaugural set of submissions. Whether you wish to examine contemporary legal debates, offer a critique of landmark cases, or explore emerging areas of law, your voice is valued.

Submission Guidelines

Submissions are welcome from students or professionals from any background or experience.

Submissions should be original, well-researched, and written in a clear, professional manner. Contributions may include:

  • Opinion pieces on pressing legal issues
  • Case law analysis & case notes
  • Legislative reviews
  • Legal history and theory

To submit your article for consideration, please contact auditor@lawsoc.ie.

Submission Instructions

Please ensure your submission aligns with the guidelines above. We encourage diverse topics related to law.
