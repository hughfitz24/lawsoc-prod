Mooting & Mock Trials

If you’ve ever dreamed of captivating a courtroom and developing your advocacy skills, Mooting and Mock Trial is the activity for you.

It teaches you real-life skills needed to be a lawyer, like analysing problems, crafting arguments, and delivering them with poise.

You also get to dress smartly, think Harvey Spectre & Danna Paulsen from Suits.

LawSoc has a storied history of success in Mooting; recent teams have placed 2nd place in the National Moot, as well as victories at recent Intervarsity moots.

LawSoc Moots & Mock Trials

Semester 1

  1. Hugh Geoghegan Novice Mock Trial sponsored by Eversheds Sutherland - October

  2. Vivian Lavan Novice Mock Trial - November

Semester 2

  1. William Fry Mock Trial - February

  2. Thomas Finlay Intervarsity Moot - March

We also send teams across the country and the UK to compete in places like Galway, Cork & Nottingham. Lawsoc subsides these trips to make them affordable… so what are you waiting for?
