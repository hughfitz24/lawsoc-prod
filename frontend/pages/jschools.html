About the Competition

LawSoc is proud to run the Junior Schools Debating Competition for another year. This competition, a long-standing tradition, is entirely organised and judged by LawSoc members.

It serves to promote competitive debating among secondary school students in 1st to 3rd Year, with weekly debates against their peers.

Our History

Founded by Catherine Duplaa, the Leinster Junior Schools Debating Competition has been shaping the future of young debaters for over 20 years.

Many successful debaters have launched their careers through this competition, some of whom have achieved success at European and World University Debating Competitions.
