Junior Schools Debating Competition

LawSoc is proud to run the Junior Schools Debating Competition for another year.

This competition is a long-standing tradition in our society, with the competition organised and judged entirely by LawSoc members.

The competition serves to promote competitve debating amongst secondary school students between 1st and 3rd Year through weekly debate against their peers.
