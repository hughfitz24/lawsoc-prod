House Debates

The cornerstone of intellectual discourse

A Storied Tradition

The weekly house debate has been the cornerstone of the Law Society throughout its existence, preserving the art of discourse and oration while discussing topics that shape our world.

How Our Debates Work

7-Minute Speeches

Each speaker delivers a structured 7-minute speech, with the middle 5 minutes open to points of information from the floor.

Structured Arguments

Three or four speakers argue for and against a set motion, presenting compelling cases from both sides of the debate.

Popular Vote

The winner is decided by a popular vote of attending members, making every voice count in determining the victor.

Packed Chamber

Our debates regularly fill the chamber, continuing our long history as a major event on the UCD campus.
