Honorary Lifetime Membership

The Honorary Life Membership is the highest distinction awarded by the Law Society of UCD, recognizing individuals who have made exceptional contributions to their field and to society. Over the years, we have had the privilege of honoring a diverse range of actors, authors, politicians, athletes, comedians, and public figures, reflecting the society’s commitment to excellence across all disciplines. Recipients of an Honorary Life Membership join a distinguished group and are granted lifelong membership in the society.

Notable recipients of Honorary Life Membership include:

Actors: Patrick J. Adams, Sir Christopher Lee, Martin Sheen, John C. McGinley, Dirk Benedict, Jeremy Irons, David Kelly, and Leslie Nielsen.

Comedians: Colin Murphy, Dan Antopolski, and Bill Bailey.

Authors & Thinkers: Tucker Max, Jung Chang, and Noam Chomsky.

Political Figures: Uachtarán na hÉireann Michael D Higgins, Ken Livingstone and Bill Clinton.

Sports Icons: Cristiano Ronaldo, Pauleta, Jimmy White, Brian O’Driscoll, and Paul O’Connell.

Cultural Icons: Dancer Michael Flatley, supermodel Erin O’Connor, and Nobel laureate Seamus Heaney.

Each recipient has left a lasting impact on their industry and beyond, embodying the values of leadership, innovation, and dedication. Many have visited UCD to speak with our members, sharing insights into their careers and experiences.

Stay tuned for future recipients and special guest events, as we continue to celebrate excellence through our Honorary Life Membership awards!
